Who are we?

Celebrating its 20 years since it was established, Safe Wildlife is an organization with the intention of conserving vulnerable wildlife species around the world from becoming extinct. We empower, educate and inspire people; encourage wildlife protection and provide funding. Our team started at 12th May and we had been working years to reach our goals.

Our Goals

Recognizing that the problems facing our planet are increasingly more complex and urgent, Safe Wildlife focuses its work on four ambitions.

Conserve wildlife and places

Protect freshwater resources and landscapes

Raise awareness of endangered wildlife

Conserve the world's most important forests

Our Impact

Safe Wildlife has been working along with other organizations to save animals on a global level. We have been through about 79 countries, conserving around 1,400 species and 5 forests. We have collaborated with local community organizations, and global organizations to persuade the public as well as working with schools to provide better education about wildlife conservation.
