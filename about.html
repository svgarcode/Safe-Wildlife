Our Mission

Our main mission is to succeed in our dreams to save endangered wild species. Throughout our history, we have never missed any opportunities to save our wildlife. Hence, I believed that we have made lots of missions. Animals should have the right to live freely, without being killed or exploited by humans.


Our Vision

Our vision is to have a clear future that the next human generation could have a chance to see almost all of the animals that we have seen. We sincerely do not want them to just see an extinct animal in a picture book since we all have experienced that feeling. We sympathize with them. Therefore, we will never let it happen.

Our History

"Safe Wildlife" was established in 2000 by a group of passionate individuals: Rubia, Violet, and Heather who sought to provide funding for plants and animals who are threatened by human development. They began by employing individuals with similar ambitions to save wildlife. Initially, they educated local people, slowly received donations and funds from local organizations, and connected with other local wildlife-conservation organizations to collaborate and look out for animals in the local forests.


After 5 years as 'Safe Wildlife', they are receiving funds on a global basis and saving animals from forests all around the world. Therefore,they decided to create a website in order to interact more with their global supporters who are willing to provide them. In 2002, the non-profit yet helpful website of 'Safe wildlife' was formed. Their story is TO BE CONTINUED.......
